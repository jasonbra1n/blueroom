About The Blue Room

The Blue Room is a private BYOB event space and lounge in the heart of old downtown historic Coboconk. Founded by Kingsley, we bring together like-minded people to celebrate live music, art, and wellness, paired with unique local catered food and fabulous entertainment.

Available for Rent: Book The Blue Room for your next private event—perfect for gatherings, celebrations, or creative showcases!

Features

  • Intimate 100-seat capacity
  • BYOB (Bring Your Own Beverage)
  • Local catered food options
  • Full bar with local brews (for select events)
  • Free parking nearby
